Biography

I am currently a forth (last) year PhD student in Zhejiang University, where I’m honored be advised by Yue Zhang. Previously, I earned my master degree from the Harbin Institute of Technology, working with Hailong Cao and Tiejun Zhao. I received my undergraduate degree on Computer Science from Harbin Institute of Technology. I also worked (as research intern) with Sougou, Alibaba DAMO Academy and Tencent AI Lab.

My research area spans from fundamental language analysis (semantics in particular) to real-world NLP applications (e.g., dialogue systems, text generation, translation).
